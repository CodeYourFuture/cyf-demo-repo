Pancakes recepe

A quick and easy recipe to make pancakes!

Ingredients

  • 175g flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 450ml milk
  • Sunflower oil

Methhod

  • Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  • Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, oil, and egg. Mix until smooth.
  • Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  • Pour or scoop batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 1 to 2 minutes.
  • Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
